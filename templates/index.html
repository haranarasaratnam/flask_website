Hello!

I'm Haran Arasaratnam. Welcome to my personal website. I obtained my MSc and PhD degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from McMaster University, completing them under Distinguished University Professor Simon Haykin. My two main research interests include Bayesian signal processing and control theory. During my doctoral studies, I developed the Cubature Kalman Filter (CKF) theory. The related contribution is regarded as seminal in the field of control theory with over 4000 citations and has been applied successfully to numerous real-world inference problems.

Here, you will find a collection of my graduate level course notes on Bayesian estimation and control theory accompanied by Matlab and Python codes. For comments and suggestions, you may reach out to me at haran.arasaratnam@icloud.com.
