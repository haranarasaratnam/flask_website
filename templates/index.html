Welcome to my personal website. I obtained my MSc and PhD degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from McMaster University, completing them under Distinguished University Professor Simon Haykin. My two main research interests include signal processing and control theory. During my doctoral studies, I developed the Cubature Kalman Filter (CKF) theory. The contribution is regarded as seminal in the field of control theory with over 5000 citations.

Here, you will find a collection of my course notes and research projects accompanied by Matlab and Python codes. Feel free to explore this website and leave your comments at haran.arasaratnam@icloud.com.
